Love Assembly Parish

About Us

Committed to Spiritual Enlightenment

Faith. Passion. Community. Worship. These are the cornerstones behind RCCG Regina Love Assembly, CA, and we approach each day with these in mind. Founded in 2000, our Church was established on the ideals of giving everyone a chance to learn, grow, and define their own religious identity through worship. Becoming a church member means joining a family who, each day, are devoted to expressing their love of God.
